序 · PraefatioPraefatio · Preface

三十万年前，智人不过是非洲草原上一种并不起眼的动物。然而凭借讲述虚构故事的独特能力，他们编织出神话、货币、帝国与科学，在短短数万年间登上食物链的顶端，又几乎握住了创造与毁灭世界的全部力量。这部编年史循「认知 · 农业 · 融合 · 科学」四场革命，逐页记下智人如何成为今日之智人——以及，将走向何方。

Three hundred thousand years ago, Sapiens was an unremarkable animal of the African savanna. Yet through the singular power to tell fictional stories, they wove myths, money, empires, and science — and within a few short millennia seized the powers to create and to destroy. This chronicle follows four revolutions — cognitive, agricultural, imperial, and scientific — to record, leaf by leaf, how Sapiens became what we are, and where we may yet be bound.
